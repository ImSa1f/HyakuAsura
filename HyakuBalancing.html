What Changed

Quick overview — everything in this update.

Combat
  • Damage decay — hit the same person too many times in a row and your damage drops. Resets when you stop attacking.
  • Multiple attacker stun decay — getting ganged up on no longer locks you in stun forever. With 3+ attackers, stun duration is cut by 3×.
  • Whiffed skill endlag — missing a skill with recovery time prevents you from immediately blocking.
  • M1 feels snappier — base M1 cooldown reduced. M2 no longer resets your M1 combo stage.
  • 4 second extender cooldown — can't chain extenders one after another with no gap.
  • One ragdoll per combo string — muscle builds can't stack Earth Shatter + Elbow Drop + Body Drop in one combo.
  • Max 2 extenders per combo.
  • Extender damage drop — each extender or grab that connects reduces the following damage by 20% (stacking).
  • Landing a punch halves your recovery — miss and you take the full penalty.
  • Walking and punching is 18% slower.
  • Lion Bite and Lightning Flash are now blockable.
  • Wall hit iframes doubled — contact 0.6→1.5s, pinned 1.0→2.5s.
  • Perfect Block window tightened for M1s — 0.5s→0.275s. Skills and Criticals remain at 0.5s.
  • Burst bar fills on every hit — grabs and 0-damage contacts now count.
  • Full Rhythm bar = up to 30% cheaper stamina on all combat moves.
  • Attack Speed hard capped at 6,000.
  • High Attack Speed = up to 40% shorter stun on your M1s at the cap.
Defense
  • Durability no longer reduces damage — gives much more HP instead.
  • Muscle gives damage reduction only — up to ~15% at 3,200 muscle.
  • Fat gives both HP and damage reduction — DR is lower than muscle (max ~8% at 2,000 fat).
Stats & Movement
  • 0 muscle start — −28% sprint speed and −35% damage. Both reach normal at 70 muscle.
  • Fat without muscle penalty increased.
  • Low HP slow hits earlier and is stronger — full effect at 40% HP.
  • Strength training gains halved at 6k+.
  • Attack Speed training gains halved at 5k+.
  • Upper/Lower Muscle training gains reduced at 3k+ — ~3% per rep → ~2%.

Combo Rules
Limits
  • One ragdoll move per combo string — the first ragdoll-causing move goes through. Everything after is blocked until the combo resets.
  • Maximum 2 extenders per string — the third one simply won't connect. Resets when combo ends.
  • Extender/grab damage drop — each extender or grab that connects makes following hits deal 20% less (stacking). Resets when combo ends.
  • Landing an M1 cuts recovery in half — whiffing gets full endlag.
  • Walking while punching is 18% slower.

Directional Dodge
  • Base distance: 58 units. Scales up with Agility (up to 2,000 Agility) and Total Muscle (up to 800 TM bonus).
  • Distance drops with each consecutive dash — spamming dashes gets you less distance each time.
  • Heavy muscle (4,000+ TM) halves the bonus distance if you've already dashed twice in a string.
  • Hard cap at 175 units per dash regardless of stats.
  • Cooldown: 2 seconds per direction. Increases to 3 seconds above 500 Total Muscle.
