What's New This Update

Quick rundown.

Combat
  • Damage decay — repeated hits drop in damage. Resets when you stop.
  • Stun decay vs multiple attackers — 3+ people can no longer permastun.
  • Missed skill endlag — can't block during the whiff punish window.
  • Lingering hitboxes — skills connect through the full swing arc.
  • M1 cooldown −0.5s, M2 no longer resets M1 combo stage.
  • Global extender cooldown 4s — no back-to-back extender spam.
  • One ragdoll per combo string — muscle builds can't stack floor-hitting moves.
  • Max 2 extenders per string — no infinite loops.
  • Extender / grab damage drop — each one that connects deals −20% after.
  • Hit-based endlag on M1 — land it: −50% recovery. Whiff: full endlag.
  • Air punch slow — M1 while walking: −18%.
  • Lion Bite & Lightning Flash now blockable — IgnoreParry and IgnorePerfectBlock removed.
  • Wall hit iframes increased — contact 0.6→1.5s, pinned 1.0→2.5s.
  • Full Rhythm bar = cheaper stamina — up to −30% all combat drains.
  • Attack speed hard capped at 6000.
  • High AS = less stun on M1s — up to −40% at max AS.
Stat / Training
  • Muscle startup — 0 muscle: −28% sprint, −35% damage. Normalises at 70.
  • Fat-without-muscle penalty increased — extra slow coefficient 0.115 → 0.175.
  • Low HP slow stronger — full penalty at 40% HP (was 35%), magnitude up.
  • Strength gains nerfed at 6k+ — halved.
  • AttackSpeed gains nerfed at 5k+ — halved.
  • Upper/Lower Muscle gains nerfed at 3k+ — ~3% → ~2%.
  • Weighted Vest — now +10% Upper/Lower Muscle gains too.
  • Breathing Mask — flat +25% Stamina gains everywhere (was bike-only).
  • Limb Weights — now +25% Agility gains too.

Movement & Attack Speed
Speed formula (sprint)
-- CRnInput sprint loop
CalculatedRunSpeed = (((19 + AgilityBonus) / HealthMultiplier) * 1.08) * 0.9
CalculatedRunSpeed *= (1 - MuscleSpeedPenalty)   -- 0-14% at 800-7000 muscle
CalculatedRunSpeed *= (1 - StartupPenalty)        -- 0-28% at 0-70 muscle
BaseWalkSpeed = CalculatedRunSpeed * FatSlow      -- fat modifier applied last
Muscle startup penalty
  • 0 muscle: −28% sprint, −35% damage. Both normalise at 70.
  • 800–7000 muscle: existing high-muscle penalty up to −14%.
Fat-without-muscle penalty
-- Active when FatVal >= 100 AND Muscle/Fat < 1
FatSlowBase = lerp(1, 0.8, clamp(FatVal/1000, 0, 1))
ExtraProgress = clamp((FatVal - 100) / 900, 0, 1)
FatSlow = FatSlowBase - ExtraProgress * 0.175  -- was 0.115
Low HP slow
-- Penalty ramps from full HP down to 40%
HealthMultiplier = 1 + (0.40 - clamp(hpRatio - 0.40, 0, 0.40)) * 2.55
-- At 100% HP: HealthMultiplier = 1.0  (no penalty)
-- At  40% HP: HealthMultiplier = 2.04 (~51% slower)
-- At   0% HP: same as 40% (max)
