What's new this pass NEW

Quick rundown. The rest of the page goes into the actual numbers.

Combat stuff

  • Damage decay — hit someone too many times in a row and your damage goes down. Resets if you stop hitting them.
  • Stun decay when ganged up on — getting jumped by 3+ people no longer locks you in stun forever.
  • Missed skill endlag — whiff a skill and you eat a bit of recovery time before you can act again.
  • Lingering hitboxes are back — skills connect through the full swing now, not just one frame.
  • M1 cooldown shorter by 0.5s — base M1 just feels snappier.
  • M2 doesn't reset M1 combo — you can throw an M2 mid-string and keep your M1 stage.
  • Global extender cooldown of 4s — can't chain three extenders back to back anymore.
  • One ragdoll move per combo string — muscle builds can't stack Earth Shatter + Elbow Drop + Body Drop in one sequence.
  • Max 2 extenders per combo string — no more infinite loop chains.
  • Extender / grab damage drop — each extender or grab that connects reduces subsequent hit damage by 20%.
  • Hit-based endlag reduction — landing an M1 cuts your recovery time in half vs. a whiff.
  • Air punch slow — M1s thrown while walking are 18% slower if you miss.
  • Wall hit iframes increased — contact iframe 0.6s→1.5s, wall-pinned iframe 1.0s→2.5s. Stops chain combos on wall-stuck targets.
  • One ragdoll per combo — bug fixed — the flag now auto-resets after 4s so pure-skill combos without M1s no longer permanently break it.
  • Muscle startup overhaul — 0 muscle now runs 28% slower and deals 35% less damage, normalising to baseline at 70 muscle.
  • Can't dash and block at the same time — pick one.
  • Land a perfect block? no cooldown — only when u mistime it does the 4s cooldown apply.
  • Full Rhythm bar = cheaper stamina — up to 30% off all combat stamina drains when your rhythm is maxed.
  • Landing a skill costs 30% less stamina than missing it — actually connect with your stuff and you save bar.
  • Attack speed hard caps at 6000 — stat won't grow past it.
  • High AS = less stun on M1s — fast hits hit fast but don't lock people up as much. Up to 40% less stun at max AS.

Stat / training stuff

  • Stat cap raised to 100k for durability. Fat cap up to 2.5k.
  • Strength gains nerfed at 6k+ NERF — dedicated decay table added; 6k–8k halved from Default values.
  • AttackSpeed gains nerfed at 5k+ NERF — 5k–6k bracket halved, 6k+ halved.
  • Upper/Lower Muscle gains nerfed at 3k+ NERF — ~3% per press → ~2% at 3k without protein.
  • Weighted Vest now boosts UpperMuscle & LowerMuscle +10% BUFF
  • Breathing Mask flat +25% StaminaInStat BUFF — bonus applies across all training, not just the bike.
  • Limb Weights +25% Agility gain BUFF
  • Hunger drains half as fast
  • Starving eats your muscle 60% slower now

Combat changes

Every gameplay-side combat change with the actual numbers.

Damage decay NEW

  • First 9 hits: full damage.
  • After that, every 9 hits in a row drops damage by another 8%.
  • Won't drop below 50%.
  • Tracked per attacker → target. Resets when you stop hitting them.

Multi-attacker stun decay NEW

Combo Restraints NEW

  • One ragdoll per combo string — the first ragdoll-inflicting move in a combo goes through; all subsequent ones in the same string are blocked at the server.
  • Max 2 extenders per combo string — third extender attempt is silently blocked. Counter resets on combo reset.
  • Extender/grab damage drop — each extender or grab that connects applies a 20% stacking damage reduction to all subsequent hits. Resets on combo reset.
  • Hit-based endlag on M1 — landing an M1 gives 50% reduced recovery time. Whiffing gets full endlag.
  • Air punch slow — M1 punches thrown while the player is walking are 18% slower.

Missing a skill = endlag NEW

If you throw a skill and don't hit anyone, you get a short recovery window. No more risk-free spam.

Wall hit iframe BUFFED

When you get launched into a wall, you now get 1.5s of iframes on contact and 2.5s when fully wall-pinned. Up from 0.6s and 1.0s respectively. Stops chain combos against pinned targets.

Stamina drain REWORKED

Everything's a percent of your max stamina now.

M1 drain

  • Base drain: 2% of your max stamina per swing.
  • More muscle / strength / fat = more drain.
  • Land it = 30% off. Miss = full cost.
  • Full rhythm bar = up to 30% extra off on top.

M2 drain

  • Base: 3.4% of max stamina.
  • Land it = 30% off just like M1.

Rhythm bar perks NEW

  • Damage boost — up to +15% at full rhythm.
  • Cheaper stamina — at full rhythm, up to 30% less on all combat drains.
  • Skill stamina: 70% paid up front. Land it = done. Whiff = pay the other 30% a couple seconds later.

Build cheat sheet

Lean / Agility

Hit-and-run skirmisher
  • Fastest sprint + swings
  • Lowest stam drain
  • Lowest damage per hit
  • Squishy

Muscle

Heavy hitter
  • Highest damage per hit
  • ~15% DR from muscle
  • ~14% slower sprint
  • More stam drain per swing

Strength

Balanced striker
  • Strong M1/M2 multiplier
  • Normal sprint
  • No lean bonus
  • Average HP

Durability

Tank
  • Eats damage
  • Double HP pool
  • Way less block drain
  • Best as hybrid
