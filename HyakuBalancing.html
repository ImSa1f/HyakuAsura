What's New This Pass

Quick rundown — the rest of the page has actual numbers.

Combat
  • Damage decay — hit someone too many times in a row and damage drops. Resets when you stop.
  • Stun decay vs multiple attackers — 3+ people can no longer lock you in stun forever.
  • Missed skill endlag — whiff a skill and you eat recovery time.
  • Lingering hitboxes — skills connect through the full swing, not just one frame.
  • M1 cooldown −0.5s — base M1 snappier.
  • M2 doesn't reset M1 combo — throw an M2 mid-string and keep your M1 stage.
  • Global extender cooldown 4s — can't chain three extenders back to back.
  • One ragdoll per combo string — muscle builds can't stack Earth Shatter + Elbow Drop + Body Drop.
  • Max 2 extenders per combo string — no infinite loop chains.
  • Extender / grab damage drop — each one that connects reduces subsequent damage by 20%.
  • Hit-based endlag reduction — landing an M1 cuts recovery in half vs a whiff.
  • Air punch slow — M1s while walking are 18% slower.
  • Wall hit iframes increased — contact 0.6s→1.5s, wall-pinned 1.0s→2.5s.
  • One ragdoll rule bug fixed — pure-skill combos no longer permanently break the flag.
  • Full Rhythm bar = cheaper stamina — up to 30% off all combat drains.
  • Attack speed hard capped at 6000.
  • High AS = less stun on M1s — up to 40% less at max AS.
Stat / Training
  • Muscle startup overhaul — 0 muscle: −28% sprint, −35% damage. Normalises at 70 muscle.
  • Strength gains nerfed at 6k+ — dedicated decay table; 6k–8k halved.
  • AttackSpeed gains nerfed at 5k+ — both brackets halved.
  • Upper/Lower Muscle gains nerfed at 3k+ — ~3% per press → ~2%.
  • Weighted Vest — now also boosts Upper/Lower Muscle +10%.
  • Breathing Mask — flat +25% StaminaInStat across all training (was bike-only).
  • Limb Weights — now also boosts Agility +25%.
  • Hunger drains 50% slower. Starvation muscle loss 60% slower.
